Our Journey

Shihab Ahmed, our founder, and CEO is an aspiring young businessperson. In the five years of his business career, he garnered commendable experience and success. As a Bangladeshi undergraduate engineering student, Mr. Shihab moved to Turkey in 2016. He has always had an entrepreneurial flair and has tried various commercial ventures in addition to his studies.

Mr. Shihab acquired Turkish citizenship through an investment scheme. Additionally, via his individual initiative, he assisted other individuals in obtaining residency permits and Turkish citizenship. Later, in 2021, he formally founded the Ottoman Group after being encouraged by the smiles of numerous customers.
